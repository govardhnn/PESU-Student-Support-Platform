Student Support Platform

Purpose

This platform aims to support PES students in non-academic issues by directing them to college counselors and providing them with articles to manage common student issues.

This is a brief description of the facilites this site displays:


  • Counselors

    PES Counselor details and location in campus is specified with their email ids and contact information.

    Know More

  • Blog

    Blogs from PES Counselors which can answer and suggest valuable tips to common issues.

    Read Blogs

  • Helpline

    Helplines to be used by students when there is a necessity to talk to professionals who can assist in situations where there is a necessity for somebody to immediately talk to.

    Visit Helpline

Disclaimer

This site does not intend to provide medical assistance.
If you/anybody is undergoing a crisis situation which needs attention, please visit the helpline.
